Medical Products

Commercial Technologies Plus supplies a comprehensive range of high-quality medical consumables designed to support hospitals, clinics, and healthcare facilities across Saudi Arabia.

Our products are sourced from trusted international manufacturers and meet strict quality, safety, and regulatory standards required in modern healthcare environments.

Our Product Categories

ICU Disposables

Critical care consumables designed for intensive care units, ensuring reliable performance in high-risk medical environments.

Operating Theatre Disposables

Sterile disposable products used during surgical procedures to ensure safety, hygiene, and operational efficiency.

Surgical Instruments

High-precision instruments designed for healthcare professionals performing diagnostic and surgical procedures.

CSSD Disposables

Products supporting Central Sterile Supply Department operations, ensuring safe sterilization and instrument management.

Hygienic Products

Infection control and hygiene products designed to maintain safe and clean healthcare environments.
