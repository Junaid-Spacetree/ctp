POWER INFRASTRUCTURE SERVICES

Power Infrastructure Services

Commercial Technologies Plus provides specialized engineering services supporting the development and maintenance of modern power transmission infrastructure across the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia. Our expert teams deliver advanced installation and infrastructure solutions including fiber optic integration, live-line crossing operations, and underground cable network development to support reliable energy systems.

Our Infrastructure Capabilities

OPGW Stringing

Professional Optical Ground Wire (OPGW) installation providing both transmission line protection and high-speed fiber optic communication infrastructure.

OPGW Retrofitting

Upgrading existing transmission lines with advanced fiber optic ground wire systems to enhance communication capabilities within power networks.

Live Line Crossing

Specialized engineering operations performed across energized transmission lines ensuring uninterrupted power flow and maximum operational safety.

Highway Crossing

Execution of safe transmission line crossings across major highway corridors using advanced protection systems and strict safety protocols.

Underground Cable Systems

Installation of high-performance underground power transmission cables supporting modern urban and infrastructure development.

Engineering Standards & Compliance

All infrastructure projects executed by Commercial Technologies Plus follow strict engineering guidelines and safety regulations to ensure reliable power transmission systems across the region.

International Engineering Standards

Projects executed according to globally recognized engineering and installation practices.

Safety Focused Operations

Advanced safety procedures implemented to protect personnel, equipment, and operational infrastructure.

Regulatory Compliance

Operations carried out according to Saudi Electricity Company (SEC) and national infrastructure standards.
