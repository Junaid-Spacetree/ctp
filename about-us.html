ABOUT US

About Commercial Technologies Plus (CTP)

Commercial Technologies Plus Trad. & Cont. Co (CTP) is a Saudi National owned Energy Development Company location in the kingdom of Saudi Arabia, Established with an objective of setting new standards of excellence in Over Head Transmission Lines, UG Cables & Renewable Energy Developments across the Kingdom.

CTP Established in 2014, from very modest beginning CTP has established itself a highly competitive and challenging Contracting field, its well proven and quality work has steered it into leading name with its client range with interest in Over Head Power Transmission, Sub station and Trading Activities.

CTP embrace business challenges and develop technology solutions and when coupled with the customer's requirements become the leading business model of the future.

Our Journey & Growth

We gained valuable grounds and earned a place for itself through hard work, dedicated efforts and a very professional and skillful approach to every project on hand, be it small medium or large.

This has resulted in securing repeated orders and additional of new customers to its growing list of clientele.

Our Technical Capabilities

Today, CTP is well equipped and staffed to handily variety of projects in the high, medium and low voltage Over Head Power Transmission networks, Electrical distribution networks, substation works and other associated electrical works.

  • Over Head Power Transmission Networks
  • Electrical Distribution Networks
  • Substation Works
  • Associated Electrical Infrastructure Projects
