Our Services

MECHANICAL, ELECTRICAL & PLUMBING (MEP)

‹ ›

Ever since its inception, CTP has significantly grown and established a reputation as a leading MEP contracting company in the K.S.A backed by a team of experienced and highly qualified professionals. As an MEP contractor CTP undertakes all activities relating to Electrical, Mechanical and Plumbing works covering large and medium scale facilities such as Commercial Buildings, Industrial Buildings, Residential Buildings, Educational Institutions and buildings
for various Government Organizations in Saudi Arabia.

The scope of activities include design, procurement, installation, testing and commissioning of the following:-

  • NEW CONSTRUCTION & REMODELING
  • ELECTRICAL PANEL INSTALLATION
  • LOW VOLTAGE/MEDIUM VOLTAGE TRANSFORMER INSTALLATION
  • CABLE TRAY INSTALLATION & CABLE PULLING
  • CABLE TERMINATION
  • LIGHTNING PROTECTION AND EARTHING SYSTEMS
  • STREET LIGHTING INSTALLATION
  • INTERIOR LIGHTING SYSTEM INSTALLATION
  • VOICE/DATA/VIDEO INSTALLTION
  • FIRE FIGHTING/FIRE ALARM SYSTEM INSTALLATION
  • RETROFITTING OF EXISTING ELECTRICAL SYSTEMS.
  • HVAC SOLUTION
  • UNDERGROUND / ABOVEGROUND DRAINAGE SYSTEMS
  • DOMESTIC COLD AND HOT WATER SUPPLY SYSTEMS INSTALLATION
  • SANITARY WARES INSTALLATION
