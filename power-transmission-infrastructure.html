POWER TRANSMISSION INFRASTRUCTURE

Power Transmission Infrastructure

Commercial Technologies Plus specializes in the development and construction of high-voltage Over Head Transmission Line (OHTL) infrastructure across the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia. Our experienced engineering teams deliver reliable transmission solutions supporting modern energy networks. With advanced equipment and strict safety standards, we execute large-scale power infrastructure projects efficiently and safely.

Our Transmission Services

Tower Foundation

Engineering and construction of strong and reliable foundations for high-voltage transmission towers, ensuring long-term structural stability.

Ground Assembly & Erection

Professional ground assembly and crane-assisted erection of transmission towers ranging from 115 kV to 500 kV capacity.

Transmission Line Stringing

Comprehensive conductor stringing services including pilot wire installation, conductor pulling, insulator assembly, and final sag adjustments.

Live Line & Highway Crossing

Execution of complex transmission line crossings across energized circuits and major highway infrastructure with strict safety controls.

OPGW Installation

Installation and retrofitting of Optical Ground Wire systems providing power line protection and fiber optic communication networks.
