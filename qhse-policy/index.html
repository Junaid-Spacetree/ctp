QHSE-POLICY

QUALITY, HEALTH, SAFETY & ENVIRONMENT

Quality

We are passionate about emphasising quality through our continuous improvement initiatives. Our entire business has been mapped against customer requirements with the aim of bolstering the culture of client delight. We have also developed and implemented real time quality management system supporting processes such as document control, audit and review, the reporting on and correction of non-conformance. We deploy competent quality engineers to each site and review the quality of our outputs for years after installation.

Health and Safety

We are deeply committed to Ensuring safety. Zero harm is our unwavering goal and all operations structures, systems and processes are designed to minimise risks in our operations. We believe that health, safety and the wellness of our people and community is integral to the sustainability and quality of outputs of our business and as such drive the culture of health and safety as a way of life for all.

Environmental management

We are devoted to Environmental leadership aim to continuously improve our contribution to the environment with an active and results oriented approach throughout all facets of our business. We promote environmental awareness throughout the value chain by sharing our successes and providing company resources to address local needs. Water recycling, sound and dust reduction campaigns are some of our initiatives.
