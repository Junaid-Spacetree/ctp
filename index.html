Why Choose Commercial Technologies Plus (CTP)

Commercial Technologies Plus Trad. & Cont. Co (CTP) is a Saudi national company specializing in infrastructure development, power transmission services, technical contracting, trading, and medical supply distribution across the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia.

Established in 2014, the company has built a strong reputation through its professional approach, technical expertise, and commitment to delivering high-quality solutions for projects of all sizes. CTP continuously focuses on innovation, safety, and operational excellence while supporting the Kingdom's growing industrial and infrastructure sectors.

With experienced teams and modern equipment, CTP successfully delivers projects in overhead transmission lines, underground cable systems, electrical infrastructure, trading services, and healthcare supply solutions while maintaining strict safety standards and reliable project execution.

Our Expertise

CTP provides a wide range of engineering, contracting, and technical services supporting infrastructure, industrial, and energy projects across Saudi Arabia. Our multidisciplinary expertise allows us to deliver reliable and efficient solutions tailored to client requirements.
